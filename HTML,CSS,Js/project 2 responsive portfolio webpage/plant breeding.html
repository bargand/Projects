GENETICS & PLANT BREEDING


Concept Of Genetics :

Genetics is a biological science which deals with the principles of heridity and variation. Heridity refers to the transmission of characters form parents to their offspring. 'Thus, genetics is a science which unrelevs the inheritance to verious characters from the generation to another'.
The term genetics was first used by Bateson(1950) that is 5 years,after its birth. The word genetics has been derived form the greek word gene, which means to become or to grow. Since characters are governed by genes, genetics is the study of stracture, composition and function of genes.


Father Of Genetics :

♦ Sir. Gregor Johann Mendel ♦


♣ The word genetics, coined by William Bateson (1906), is derived from the Latin word genesis, which means birth.


Definition :

✔ Genetics is the study of heredity and variation.

✔ The scientific study of the way that the development of living things is controlled by qualities that have been passed on from parents to children.


✔ Genetics is defined as the branch of biology that deals with the study of genetic variation, genes, and heredity.


✔ Gregor Mendel, through his work on pea plants, discovered the fundamental laws of inheritance. He deduced that genes come in pairs and are inherited as distinct units, one from each parent. Mendel tracked the segregation of parental genes and their appearance in the offspring as dominant or recessive traits.
